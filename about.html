Anthony Applegate

Anthony Dayne Applegate is a visual artist, writer and filmmaker. He wrote the award-winning short film The Lemonade Trade. In 2013, along with his brother, he co-founded Stolen Runabout Studios, a multimedia production company in which he serves as the Chief Creative Officer. He has also worked on the production side; producing, directing, editing and helping with numerous film projects that have been accepted into various festivals.
